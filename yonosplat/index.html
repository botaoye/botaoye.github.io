YoNoSplat

YoNoSplat: You Only Need One Model for
Feedforward 3D Gaussian Splatting

Botao Ye1,2     Boqi Chen1,2     Haofei Xu1     Daniel Barath1     Marc Pollefeys1,3
1ETH Zurich     2ETH AI Center     3Microsoft    
Paper Code Release: Dec 2025

YoNoSplat reconstructs 3D Gaussian splats directly from unposed and uncalibrated images, while flexibly leveraging ground-truth camera poses or intrinsics when available.

The project page is still under construction (stay tuned!).

Pose-free, feed-forward novel view synthesis on unseen scenes with varying numbers of input views.
